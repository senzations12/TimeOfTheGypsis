Ivo Andric

Ivan "Ivo" Andric (1892 - 1975) was a Yugoslav novelist, short story
writer, and the 1961 winner of the Nobel Prize in Literature. His
writings dealt mainly with life in his native Bosnia under the Ottoman
Empire. His native house in Travnik has been transformed into a
Museum, and his Belgrade flat on Andricev Venac hosts the Museum of
Ivo Andric, and Ivo Andric Foundation.
